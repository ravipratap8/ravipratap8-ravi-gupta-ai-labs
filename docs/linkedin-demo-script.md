# LinkedIn Demo Script (60–90 seconds)

**Hook** — "Most teams bolt AI onto support and hope for the best. As a Test Manager, I built the opposite: AI with governance baked in."

1. **Landing** (`/`): "This is my portfolio — 19+ years in quality engineering, now building AI products."
2. **Dashboard** (`/dashboard`): "AI EventOps Assistant for event organisers. Note the AI Safety status — auto-send is *disabled*."
3. **AI Inbox**: "A customer asks about parking. The AI drafts a grounded reply with a confidence score — but it does not send."
4. **Approvals**: "Here is the human-in-the-loop. Refunds and complaints are flagged High risk and prioritised. I approve, edit or reject."
5. **AI Content**: "One click turns an event into an Instagram caption, reel script or email."
6. **Governance**: "And this is the part I care about most — a prompt risk register, test scenarios and audit logs. This is how you ship AI responsibly."

**Close** — "AI that is helpful *and* governed. If you are hiring for senior quality, delivery or AI leadership in NZ/AU, let&#39;s talk."
